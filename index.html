2025 Speaker Lineup

This year's tech convention features an inspiring lineup of speakers who are shaping the future of innovation. From artificial intelligence and sustainability to cybersecurity, each expert brings unique insights into the technologies transforming our world. Attendees will gain fresh perspectives, practical knowledge, and a glimpse into the next wave of digital progress.

Dr. Maya Lin

Dr. Maya Lin is a Stanford AI researcher focused on ethical machine learning. Her talk explores how technology can make fair, transparent decisions that reflect human values.

Sofia Ramirez

Sofia Ramirez is the co-founder of EcoTech Labs, a startup blending sustainability with tech innovation. She'll discuss how data and automation can reduce waste and drive greener industries.

Jordan Chen

Jordan Chen is a cybersecurity strategist and former Google engineer. His session highlights key trends in digital privacy and practical ways to stay safe online.
